Your Partner for Innovative Medicines

Adianant is a leading provider of clinical trial supply solutions
for pharmaceutical and biotech companies worldwide.

At Adianant, we understand that clinical trial supply is challenging for pharma
and biotech companies, with the possibility for financial risk, delay and exposure.

We will support the Sponsor in its forecast and planning of clinical trial supply needs.
Our focus is to bring the medicine to the patient in the trial.

Sourcing

Sourcing is one of our core capabilities, and we go above and beyond to provide comprehensive services - from strategic supply assessments to procurement, logistics and documentation.

We source the following clinical trial supplies, including but not limited to: comparators, pre-medication, co-medication, rescue medication, other ancillaries, …

Storage and Distribution

We store, handle and distribute your (n)-IMP and materials for Clinical Trials.

Within our GDP storage facility we can efficiently meet your clinical supply needs.

We partner with audited logistic professional companies throughout the world.

Project Management

Our project team will collaborate, consult and connect with you to ensure your clinical trial is managed succesfully from start to finish.

We are well connected in the clinical trial landscape (CRO, CDMO, ... ) to coordinate your trial on a global scale.
